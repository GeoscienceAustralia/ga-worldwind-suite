Pan:
  • Left mouse button click & drag - all directions
  • Left/Right/Up/Down arrows on the keyboard
  • Left mouse button click once to center view
Zoom:
  • Use the scroll wheel on the mouse
  • Plus (+) and Minus (-) on the keyboard
  • Left/Right mouse button double click to zoom in/out and center
  • Middle mouse button click & drag - up and down
Tilt:
  • Right mouse button click & drag - up and down
  • Page Up and Page Down on the keyboard
  • Hold Shift on the keyboard and Left mouse button click & drag - up and down
  • Hold Shift and Up/Down arrows on the keyboard
Rotate:
  • Right mouse button click & drag - left and right
  • Hold Shift on the keyboard and Left mouse button click & drag - left and right
  • Hold Shift and Left/Right arrows on the keyboard
Stop:
  • Spacebar
Reset heading:
  • N
Reset all:
  • R
